Privacy Policy

Overview

Life Daily Quotes is a mobile application designed to inspire users with daily motivational, life, love, wisdom and more quotes. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how information is handled within the app.

Information We Collect

Life Daily Quotes does not require account registration. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app may collect anonymous usage data and device information to improve performance and stability.

Favorites & Local Storage

When you save quotes to favorites, the data is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or store your favorites on external servers.

Screenshots & Shared Images

The app displays images and quote backgrounds for visual inspiration. Screenshots created for sharing or previews are processed locally on your device and are not collected or stored by us.

Advertising & Analytics

Life Daily Quotes may use third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect anonymous identifiers to display relevant ads and analyze app usage.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

Audience's Privacy

Life Daily Quotes does not collect personal information from any intended audience.
